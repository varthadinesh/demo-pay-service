About OnTmPay

Setu means bridge. That's what we build between Bharat's counters and its money.

OnTmPay started with a simple observation: a retail shopkeeper in a district town was running four different apps to collect a mobile recharge, an electricity bill, a school fee and an insurance premium — each with a different login, a different settlement cycle, and a different support number. We put all of it behind one counter screen and one wallet.

Today our three-tier network — super distributors at the state level, distributors at the district level, and retailers at the counter — moves payments across BBPS-listed billers, education institutions, insurers, and ferry operators, with commission settling the moment a transaction clears.

Our mission

Put every everyday payment — bills, fees, premiums, bookings — within reach of a five-minute counter visit, anywhere in the country.

How we work

We route transactions through licensed banking and BBPS rails; OnTmPay itself is the counter layer, not a bank or a bill aggregator.

Who we serve

Super distributors and distributors building a retail network, and retailers who want more reasons for a customer to walk in.
